# Research Guidelines (Public Repository)

> Author: [Dr Donghwan Shin](https://dshin.info) | Senior Lecturer @ School of Computer Science, University of Sheffield

This public repository contains research guidelines for students at various levels, from UG dissertation to MSc thesis and PhD thesis. These guidelines are designed to help students navigate the research process effectively and produce high-quality academic work.

It includes folders for various aspects of research, such as:
- Paper reading
- Paper writing
- Guidelines for PhD students (applicable to UG/MSc research students as well)
- LaTeX examples for different types of academic documents

Feel free to explore the repository and utilise the resources provided to enhance your research skills and academic writing. If you have any ideas for improvement or additional resources that could be included, please don't hesitate to contribute (e.g. make a Pull Request) or reach out to me. 

Enjoy your research journey!
